AI – a game changer in the field of Medicine.
Artificial Intelligence (AI) has entrenched a major upheaval in most of the industries today. It has also infiltrated into the realm of health care sector at a breakneck speed. The CEO of Facebook Mark Zuckerberg, has said, “With AI, especially, I’m really optimistic. In the next five to ten years, AI is going to deliver many improvements in the quality of our lives”. One of the trending domains under AI is Radiology. There is huge gap between supply and demand Curve especially with respected to the available radiologists. AI influences automatic diagnosis. Ample data is available to implement AI technology to make life easy. Radiology is the first Food and Drug Authority (FDA) approved AI/ML application. Cardiology is another category that has received four FDA-approved medical devices and algorithms. AI/ML applications are used for medical devices apart from these there is huge scope for laboratory result interpretation tool in the field of internal medicine especially for diabetes. The ability to intensify automatic detection and the support to intelligent decision-making makes AI a game-changer. Eventually AI will bring about another revolution in health care sector. Dr. Nitin Rakesh HoD – Computer Science And Engineering, School Of Engineering and Technology, Sharda University.

National Cadet Corps
NCC Naval Wing Cadets celebrate Indian Coast Guard day on the 1st of February every year since 1977, with the enactment of Coast Guard Act, ICG was declared as a full-fledged independent Armed Force under the Union Ministry of Defense. National Cadet Crops Naval Wing of India was started with a mission to groom the youth of the country as disciplined and patriotic citizens, with the motto ‘Unity and Discipline‘. NCC Naval Wing students undergo vigorous training under various formats including Ship-Modeling, Swimming Range Firing (.22’’), drill, tent Pitching, Semaphore, and Rigging. Joining NCC- Naval Wing gives students the great experience of sailing in ships. They also get a chance to represent State in the Inter Directorate Competitions and at the Republic day camp held at New Delhi, they also participate in National Level Camps, Mountaineering, Trekking & Skiing activities. This intense training helps them to participate in various competitions and Nation Building Community Services. The Merit holders with “C” Certificate can apply for direct SSB (Service Selection Board) interviews. These students are given first preference by the State government in all the Civil Service Recruitments. Indian Navy has a special quota for the NCC- Naval Wing Cadets to apply for Executive cadre or any other Senior positions with a “C” Certificate. We salute all our Indian Coast Guards who protect and safeguard our Maritime border. Dr. Ankur Choudhary Professor School Of Engineering and Technology, Sharda University.

Sharda University won the Award for “Excellence in Globalization of Education in India” at the FICCI Higher Education Excellence Summit 2022 23 Nov 2022 It is a great pleasure to announce that Sharda University was the winner of the “Excellence in Globalization of Education Award” at the 8th FICCI Higher Education Excellence Awards 2022 international education summit held in New Delhi on the 17th of November 2022. The prestigious Award that recognizes Sharda Universitys fruitful journey and its contribution to the Internationalization of Education in India was bestowed to Sharda University that was represented by Dr. Ashok Daryani, Director International Relations, by the Guest of Honour his Excellency Dr. Bhagwat Karad, the Honourable Minister of State for Finance, Government of India, who blessed the occasion with his presence and Mr. Arun Chawla, Director General of FICCI. Sharda University was the only university awarded in the category of “Excellence in Globalization of Education”. In this Years competition, several renowned high-ranked institutes and universities blessed the FICCI Higher Education Summit with their presence, among them Manipal University, NIIT University, IIIT Delhi, Banasthali Vidyapith University, Symbiosis International University, Sanskriti University, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, and several other.

Dr. Nihar Ranjan Roy--Professor , Computer Science And Engineering, Sharda University
